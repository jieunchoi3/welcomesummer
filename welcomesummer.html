Hear the Sea - Red Velvet

I discovered this song thanks to my friend Seoyoung. As soon as I played it, I knew from the bottom of my heart that this was "my song". The calm vibes created by the gentle piano melodies and soothing vocals reminded me of the serene image of a moonlit sea, where the gentle waves shimmer and reflect the moon's radiant light.

Welcome Summer - Yoon Jong Shin

Are you into city pop? Then this is the perfect song for you to jam to during this summer. By just listening to it, you'll be transported to a sun-soaked sandy beach, with the cool breeze gently brushing against your skin, and a refreshing glass of lemonade in your hand.

Childhood - Sunmi

If you prefer a more slow-paced song and dislike loud beats, I highly recommend "Childhood" by Sunmi. Although the Korean title captures its essence even better, you can simply search for "Childhood by Sunmi" to find it. You can also click on the provided link to watch the lyrics video. This beautiful track evokes a sense of nostalgia and transports you to a realm of emotions. It has the power to make you reminisce about a long-forgotten lover, someone you may not have even had
